What is EduCorps?

Study Smarter.
Not Harder.

EduCorps is an AI-first revision platform built specifically for IGCSE students. It combines intelligent note management, topic-based practice questions, full past paper simulation, and instant AI marking across Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Economics, and Computer Science — all in one seamless interface.

Unlike generic revision tools, EduCorps understands the IGCSE curriculum, speaks the language of examiners, and gives feedback that actually pushes your grade higher across every subject.

Why choose us

A Different
Kind of Revision

01
Examiner-Grade Feedback

Our AI is trained on mark schemes, examiner reports, and real grade boundaries across all five IGCSE subjects. It marks like a real examiner — telling you exactly what a top-band response needs to score full marks.

02
Built for IGCSE

Purpose-built for IGCSE — covering Maths, Physics, Chemistry, Economics, and Computer Science across all major exam boards including Cambridge (CIE) and Edexcel. Five subjects, one platform.

03
Instant QuickMark

Photograph any handwritten answer — from a Maths proof to a Physics calculation or a Chemistry explanation — and get detailed AI marking in seconds. Works with any handwriting. Perfect for timed practice without waiting for a teacher.

04
Timed Exam Simulation

Recreate real exam conditions with auto-timed sessions. When you finish, see a full grade report with scores per question, time charts, and detailed AI feedback on every answer.

05
Track Your Progress

Every result is stored, graphed, and analysed. See how your scores evolve over time, identify which question types cost you marks, and get AI-recommended topics to focus on next.

06
Always Available AI

Stuck at midnight before your exam? The AI assistant is available 24/7 inside every section — explain Physics concepts, debug Computer Science logic, walk through Chemistry equations, or analyse Economics diagrams step by step.
